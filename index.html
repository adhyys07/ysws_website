WHAT IS THIS ?

Blender, relating to the artistic tool, in this competetion you can show off your OCs and their beautiful designs through a playable tech demo for everyone to play!!! (You will also recieve something to fuel yhe artist in you. Ssshhhhh)

REQUIREMENTS

  • Minimum 25 hours of work for developing character
  • Minimum 7 animations e.g-idle,walk,attack etc.
  • Make 1 demo video explaining your character and your game
  • The character should be original
  • You need to log your time on the blender otherwise it will not be counted
  • For developing game you can use any game engine e.g Gamemaker , Godot etc.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

How do I Join?

Click on the SIGNUP button above, fill out the form and join the slack community and start logging your time on Blender

How do I Log My time ?

Click the GO TO BLENDER button and start logging your time. Once you are finished give the description of session and upload the video of your progress

What Projects Are Allowed ?

All 2D or 3D characters with at least 7 animations and the character should be unique

Can I Do This In Team ?

No, team entries are not allowed you have to do it individually !

What Do You Get ?

If you meet all the criterias then hack club will grant you an aesprite and if you log more hours mentioned in requirements then hack club will ship you a graphic tablet

Do I Need Any Experience ?

No, its a learning experience for all of us, so no prior experience is required
